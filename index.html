Tools I Use

Unity

Unity is a cross-platform game engine developed by Unity Technologies. It was originally released in 2005 for the Xbox, and has since been developed by many companies to power their games.

Blender

Blender is a free and open-source 3D computer graphics software product created by the Blender Foundation, a non-profit organization based in Amsterdam, Netherlands.

Gravity Sketch

Gravity Sketch is a free 3D virtual reality software product created by the Gravity Sketch Foundation, an organization based in London, UK.

Roblox

Roblox is a free 3D game engine created by the Roblox Corporation, a company based in San Mateo, California.
